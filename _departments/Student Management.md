<p><strong>Vision</strong>
</p>
<p>To be a nurturing pillar of the school community that empowers every student
to grow in character, lead with integrity, and embrace their future with
confidence.</p>
<p><strong>Mission</strong>
</p>
<p>We develop the mind, body, and soul of every Rosythian by integrating
proactive wellbeing, educative discipline, and leadership opportunities
that inspire a lifelong commitment to learning and service.</p>
<p>&nbsp;<strong>Our Approach</strong>
</p>
<p><strong>1.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Character-Building Through a Positive &amp; Proactive Approach to Discipline</strong>
</p>
<p></p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 60%;" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/SM001.jpg">
</div>
<p></p>
<p>In Rosyth, we want to foster a safe and orderly environment through an
educative disciplinary process that empowers students to take responsibility
for their actions and grow into respectful, future-ready individuals. In
this process, we guide students to understand the impact of their choices
and develop better decision-making skills. We work collaboratively with
students and parents to address the root causes and build the skills needed
for positive behaviour. Our focus is on teaching the school’s ICARE values
through meaningful learning experiences<strong>.</strong>
</p>
<ol start="2" data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p><strong>Student Wellbeing and Safety</strong>
</p>
</li>
</ol>
<p>Your child's emotional, social, and mental wellbeing is our priority.
We provide counselling support, peer mentoring programmes, and early intervention
when needed. We take a firm stance against bullying and hurtful behaviour,
with comprehensive support systems in place for all students involved.</p>
<p>&nbsp;<strong>3. Student Development &amp; Personal Growth</strong>
</p>
<p>We envision Rosythians to grow up as leaders in their own lives as they
take on different leadership roles in their development, set goals to pursue
their dreams and value the talents within themselves, thereby blossoming
into confident and fulfilled individuals.</p>
<p><strong>Our Key Programmes</strong>
</p>
<p><strong>Anti-bullying Efforts</strong>
</p>
<p>Creating a safe and supportive learning environment is fundamental to
our students’ well-being. Our comprehensive approach to addressing hurtful
behaviours and bullying centres on building a strong culture of care across
the school community, supported by proactive upstream efforts, timely and
responsive interventions and sustained follow-through to support every
child.</p>

<p><strong>The Catch-Them-Right Movement</strong>
</p>
<p></p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 30%;" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/SM004.jpg">
</div>
<p>Beyond providing support and guidance, we actively nurture a culture of
affirmation and peer encouragement through our “Catch Them Right” initiative.
We believe that positive reinforcement is essential for character growth,
and this platform empowers students to proactively recognise and celebrate
the kindness and integrity shown by their peers. By shifting the focus
to these everyday moments of excellence, we foster a vibrant, uplifting
school environment where students are inspired to model the ICARE values
and build one another up with kindness, appreciation and respect.</p>
<p><strong>Parent Partnership</strong> We believe that working together with
parents is essential for your child's development. We provide regular communication
about your child's progress, resources to support positive behaviour at
home, and opportunities for consultation when needed.</p>
