</li>
<li>
<p><a href="https://www.moe.gov.sg/financial-matters/fees/egiro" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">MOE GIRO scheme</a>:
Parents can apply for the scheme, which provides a convenient and hassle-free
arrangement, and eliminate the need for your child to bring cash to school.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><a href="https://go.gov.sg/rs-wait-list-application" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Waitlist Application Form</a>:
Parents can complete and submit by 1 November 2026. Please note that vacancies
are rare and limited. Admission via the waitlist is at the discretion of
the school.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><a href="https://go.gov.sg/application-form-for-leave-of-absence" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Leave of Absence</a>:
Parents can apply and submit for your child to accompany you on overseas
postings. This will allow the child to maintain links with Singapore and
retain their existing school places.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><a href="https://go.gov.sg/moe-efasggas" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">MOE Financial Assistance Scheme</a>:
The objective of the MOE Financial Assistance Scheme (MOE FAS) is to help
financially needy Singaporean students in Government and Government-aided
schools to meet basic schooling expenses.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Parents can access the Student Details Form (SDF) portal <a href="https://pg.moe.edu.sg/forms/sdf" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Student Details Form | Ministry of Education</a> throughout
the school term using your Singpass account to update your child's address.&nbsp;
If you are unable to access the SDF portal, please complete the change
of address form <a href="https://file.go.gov.sg/addupdateform.pdf" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">addupdateform.pdf</a> and
submit to the school via <a href="mailto:rosyth_sch@schools.gov.sg" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">rosyth_sch@schools.gov.sg</a> ,
together with a copy of either parent's NRIC showing the updated address.
Parents' contact details and email addresses can also be updated through
the SDF portal.</p>
